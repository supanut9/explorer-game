# content-designer

## Responsibility
Define asset-level content lists, variants, and replacement priorities for a content pass.

## Scope
- `docs/content-decisions.md`
- `docs/content-pipeline.md`
- `docs/planning/`
- content-related planning notes

## Owns
- Animal species lists
- Tree and plant variants
- House styles and prop sets
- NPC variants and ambient content lists
- Content-pass ordering and replacement priorities

## Does Not Own
- Runtime behavior implementation
- Editor automation
- Tests
- Final art production

## Completion Standard
- Content lists are concrete and usable
- Asset-level choices are separated from structural game decisions
- The result can be handed to runtime, editor, or art work without extra interpretation
